About Dr Olaosebikan

Dr Olaosebikan provides specialist care in pain, arthritis, autoimmune and rheumatology conditions in Lagos. This page gives an overview of his clinical focus, approach to patient care, and the kinds of symptoms and conditions that may benefit from specialist review.

Professional focus

The clinic focuses on the assessment and management of joint pain, inflammatory arthritis, autoimmune disease, musculoskeletal symptoms, and related long-term care needs. The aim is to provide thoughtful, evidence-based evaluation together with practical treatment planning and follow-up support.

Conditions commonly seen

Rheumatoid arthritis

Assessment and long-term treatment planning for inflammatory joint disease.

Gout and crystal arthritis

Specialist care for flare-ups, diagnosis, and prevention of repeated attacks.

Lupus and autoimmune disorders

Review of autoimmune symptoms, diagnosis, monitoring, and ongoing care.

Joint pain and stiffness

Evaluation of persistent pain, swelling, stiffness, and reduced mobility.

How diagnosis is approached

Diagnosis begins with a careful review of symptoms, medical history, and physical examination. When relevant, previous test results and further investigations may be considered. Accurate diagnosis matters because many rheumatology and autoimmune conditions can present in similar ways, yet need different forms of care and follow-up.

Treatment approach

Treatment plans are guided by the nature of the condition, severity of symptoms, disease activity, and the patient’s broader health needs. Care may involve patient education, symptom control, long-term monitoring, lifestyle guidance, and medical treatment where appropriate. The overall goal is to improve function, reduce disease burden, and support long-term wellbeing.

When to book

It is worth seeking specialist review if you have persistent joint pain, recurrent swelling, prolonged morning stiffness, symptoms suggestive of autoimmune disease, or an established rheumatology condition that needs ongoing follow-up and treatment adjustment.
