About Dr Olaosebikan

Dr Olaosebikan provides specialist care in pain, arthritis, autoimmune and rheumatology conditions in Lagos. This page gives an overview of his approach to patient care, areas of interest, and when to consider booking an appointment.

Professional focus

The clinic focuses on the assessment and management of joint pain, inflammatory arthritis, autoimmune disease, musculoskeletal symptoms, and related long-term care needs. The goal is to provide thoughtful, evidence-based evaluation and practical treatment plans.

Conditions commonly seen

  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Gout and crystal-related joint conditions
  • Lupus and other autoimmune disorders
  • Chronic joint pain and stiffness
  • Inflammatory and non-inflammatory musculoskeletal complaints

How diagnosis is approached

Diagnosis begins with a careful review of symptoms, medical history, physical examination, and where necessary, interpretation of previous test results or further investigations. Accurate diagnosis is important because many rheumatology and autoimmune conditions can present in similar ways.

Treatment approach

Treatment plans are guided by the nature of the condition, severity of symptoms, disease activity, and the patient’s broader health needs. Care may involve education, symptom control, long-term monitoring, lifestyle guidance, and medical treatment where appropriate.

When to book

It is worth seeking specialist review if you have persistent joint pain, recurrent swelling, prolonged morning stiffness, suspected autoimmune symptoms, or an established rheumatology condition that needs ongoing follow-up.
