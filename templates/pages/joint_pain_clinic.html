Joint Pain and Stiffness Clinic in Lagos

Joint pain and stiffness can affect comfort, movement, and daily function. Some causes are temporary, while others need careful diagnosis and longer-term care. Specialist review helps identify the likely cause and the right next steps.

What this page covers

This clinic page is for patients with persistent joint pain, swelling, stiffness, reduced mobility, or joint symptoms that may be linked to arthritis, inflammation, autoimmune disease, gout, or other musculoskeletal conditions.

Common symptoms

  • Pain in one or more joints
  • Morning stiffness
  • Swelling, redness, or warmth
  • Reduced range of movement
  • Symptoms that affect walking, gripping, or daily tasks

How diagnosis is made

Diagnosis begins with symptom history and physical examination. The pattern, duration, distribution, and severity of symptoms often help distinguish between inflammatory and non-inflammatory causes of joint pain.

Treatment approach

Treatment depends on the cause. It may include pain control, management of inflammation, disease monitoring, activity guidance, and longer-term plans for chronic conditions.

When to book

Book if your joint pain has lasted beyond a short period, keeps returning, is associated with swelling or stiffness, or is affecting your quality of life.
