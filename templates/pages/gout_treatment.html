Gout Treatment in Lagos

Gout is a painful joint condition that can cause sudden attacks of swelling, redness, and severe discomfort. Specialist care can help confirm the cause, relieve symptoms, and reduce future flare-ups.

What is gout?

Gout is a form of inflammatory arthritis linked to uric acid crystal deposits in the joints. It often affects one joint at a time, especially the big toe, but can also affect the ankles, knees, feet, wrists, and elbows.

Common symptoms

  • Sudden severe joint pain
  • Redness and swelling around the joint
  • Warmth and tenderness
  • Pain that may wake you from sleep
  • Repeated attacks over time

How diagnosis is made

Diagnosis is based on the pattern of symptoms, medical history, examination, and in some cases supporting investigations. Specialist review helps separate gout from other causes of joint pain and swelling.

Treatment approach

Treatment may include managing flare-ups, preventing future attacks, identifying contributing factors, and long-term strategies for patients who have recurrent gout or more complicated disease.

When to book

Book an appointment if you have sudden attacks of severe joint pain, recurrent swelling, or if you have previously been told you have gout and need specialist assessment or follow-up.
