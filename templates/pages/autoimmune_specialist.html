Autoimmune Disease Specialist in Lagos

Autoimmune diseases can present in many ways, including joint pain, fatigue, rashes, swelling, and symptoms affecting different parts of the body. Specialist evaluation can help clarify the diagnosis and guide appropriate long-term care.

What are autoimmune diseases?

Autoimmune diseases develop when the immune system mistakenly attacks the body’s own tissues. These conditions may affect the joints, skin, muscles, blood vessels, or other organs, and they often require careful monitoring over time.

Common symptoms

  • Persistent fatigue
  • Joint pain and swelling
  • Muscle aches
  • Unexplained rashes
  • Symptoms that affect more than one body system

How diagnosis is made

Diagnosis often involves a combination of history, symptom pattern, physical examination, and review of appropriate tests. Because autoimmune diseases can mimic other illnesses, specialist assessment is valuable in narrowing the diagnosis.

Treatment approach

Management focuses on controlling inflammation, reducing flares, monitoring organ involvement, and improving quality of life. Treatment plans are tailored to the type of disease and the patient’s needs.

When to book

Book if you have symptoms suggestive of autoimmune disease, recurrent unexplained inflammation, or an existing diagnosis that needs ongoing specialist review.
