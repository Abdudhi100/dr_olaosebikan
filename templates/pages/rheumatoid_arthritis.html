Rheumatoid Arthritis Treatment in Lagos

Rheumatoid arthritis is an autoimmune condition that can cause joint pain, swelling, stiffness, and progressive damage if not treated early. Specialist assessment can help confirm the diagnosis and guide long-term care.

What is rheumatoid arthritis?

Rheumatoid arthritis is a chronic inflammatory disease in which the immune system mistakenly attacks the joints. It can affect daily function, mobility, and over time may lead to joint damage if it is not properly managed.

Common symptoms

  • Morning stiffness that lasts a long time
  • Swollen, warm, or painful joints
  • Pain in the hands, wrists, feet, or knees
  • Fatigue and reduced energy
  • Difficulty with movement or daily tasks

How diagnosis is made

Diagnosis depends on a detailed clinical assessment. This may include symptom review, examination of the affected joints, and consideration of previous investigations or additional tests where needed. Early diagnosis is important because treatment can help protect joint function.

Treatment approach

Treatment may involve patient education, medicines to control inflammation, monitoring of disease activity, support for mobility and function, and long-term follow-up tailored to the individual patient’s needs.

When to book

Book an appointment if you have persistent joint swelling, prolonged morning stiffness, pain in multiple joints, or if you have already been diagnosed and need specialist review or follow-up.
